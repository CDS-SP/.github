 ## Cheng's Data Service

  > Data engineering and quantitative research for teams that need
  > reproducible analysis, not dashboards.

  Cheng's Data Service is a small Toronto practice. We do two things:

  1. **Consulting** — machine learning models, statistical analysis,
     forecasting, and data pipelines, delivered with reproducible code
     and methodology your team can maintain after handover.
  2. **DoubleTrends™** — a public, rules-based oversold-reversal signal
     on the S&P 500 for ETF investors. Full track record, full
     methodology, open at [doubletrends.com](https://doubletrends.com).

  The signal is also the proof. If you want to see how we reason about
  data before you hire us, the [Academy](https://doubletrends.com/academy)
  is the long version.

  ### What we publish — and what we don't

  The methodology, the historical signal list, and the research code are
  public. The exact production parameters of the DoubleTrends™ model
  (lookback windows, smoothing constants, thresholds) are not. The
  [Academy](https://doubletrends.com/academy) explains *what* the
  indicators measure and *why* they work, not the specific tuned values.
